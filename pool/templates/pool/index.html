About PoolDrawResult

PoolDrawResult is a weekly UK football pool results service covering every match on the standard pool coupon — from the Premier League and Championship down to League One, League Two, and the Scottish Premiership. Each Saturday, we publish the full week's results with score draws and scoreless draws clearly marked, so pool players can scan the coupon at a glance and check their lines in seconds.

Frequently Asked Questions About Pool Draws

What is a pool draw?
A pool draw is a football match that ends in a tie that was among the pool fixtures for that week— either a score draw (both teams score, like 1–1 or 2–2) or a scoreless draw (0–0). In UK football pools, players attempt to predict which matches on the weekly coupon will end in draws, and correctly identifying enough of them wins the pool.
What's the difference between a score draw and a scoreless draw?
A score draw is any drawn match where both teams score — 1–1, 2–2, 3–3, and so on. A scoreless draw is specifically 0–0. Most traditional UK pools only pay out on score draws, which is why our results page lists them separately.
When are pool results released each week?
Pool results are released every match day evening after the final matches on the coupon finish. At PoolDrawResult, we update the page live as results come in, so you can check progress throughout the day.
What do EKO and LKO mean on the pool coupon?
EKO stands for Early Kick-Off and LKO stands for Late Kick-Off. These are matches that kick off outside the standard Saturday 3pm window — typically Friday evening, Saturday lunchtime, or Saturday evening games. They're marked separately because they often affect how bookmakers and pool operators time the coupon.
How many draws usually happen in a week?
A typical UK pool coupon produces between 10 and 18 draws per week, with roughly 70–80% of those being score draws. Weeks with fewer than 8 draws are considered low-draw weeks, while anything above 20 is unusually high.
How do I use past pool results to make predictions?
Many pool players study recent form, head-to-head records, and historical draw patterns for specific fixtures to inform their selections. Our pool predictions page publishes our weekly picks, and our archive lets you review past weeks to spot patterns. Remember — no prediction method is guaranteed, so always play responsibly.
Can I check results from previous weeks?
Yes. Use the week selector at the top of the results table to browse any week from the current season, or visit our archive page for older seasons.
